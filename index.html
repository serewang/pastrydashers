Welcome to the Pastry Dashers website! We sell delicious macarons in all sorts of flavors. Check out our menu page to see all the different flavors that we have. You can also check out our company page to learn more about us, where we came from and how we make our macarons. We also have a page listing all our locations and a contact page with our information. Feel free to contact us if you have any questions or comments!


Our cafes are open Monday thru Saturday from 10am to 9pm, and 10am to 8pm on Sundays. Feel free to pop in to buy a pastry and some coffee, or set up your workspace at our many available cafe tables. Our cafes are furnished for your comfort, with regular chairs as well as sofas covered in pillows for you to relax in. We look forward to seeing you in person! Masks are required.


We are also active on social media! Our handles on Instagram and Twitter are @pastrydashersco. We host giveaways for giftcards and pastries so be sure to check our socials often! We also love seeing our customers and like to show them off, so please tag us in pictures you take at our cafes!
